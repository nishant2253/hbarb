■ Why MockDEX Exists — The Testnet Problem
Understanding the gap and the solution

Your TradeAgent blueprint describes a full agent lifecycle where every BUY/SELL decision is logged to HCS, then
executed on SaucerSwap DEX. On mainnet this works perfectly. On testnet it breaks at Step 23 — because
SaucerSwap, HeliSwap, and every real Hedera DEX only exists on mainnet. No liquidity pools exist on testnet.
■■ Without MockDEX: the full 34-step blueprint demo crashes at Step 23 every time. Your agent decides
BUY, logs to HCS perfectly, then has nowhere to send the trade. The demo dies and the HCS → Trade →

MockDEX Smart Contract
Complete Solidity contract · AMM formula · HCS linkage · Hedera precompiles

The MockDEX contract is the core of this entire solution. It does four things that matter for the TradeAgent
blueprint: simulates AMM swap math, stores the HCS sequence number inside every swap (creating the on-chain
proof chain), uses Hedera's Exchange Rate Precompile for USD pricing, and emits events that Mirror Node
indexes for the dashboard.
